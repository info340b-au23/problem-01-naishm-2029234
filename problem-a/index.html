Snoopy

Snoopy is a fictional dog from the comic strip Peanuts that ran from 1950 to 2000. He is known as having a vivid imagination with his constant and wild daydreams. He is best friends with the comics main character Charlie Brown and often supports him and his adventures. Snoopy is often remembered with his iconic red doghouse that he likes to sleep ontop of.
